What is Growth Device
Growth Device is a narrative-driven, text-and-GUI only, base management simulation, idle game where you play the role of a fill-in, remote Chief Officer to an undercover intelligence agency. Your job is to interact with your staff via a dedicated chat system, manage base operations, send agents to the field to recover important materials, all in an attempt to unveil a mystery that looms over the entire world.
INTERACTIVE DIALOGUE
The chat system is the first and foremost element to drive the narrative in Growth Device. It runs in parallel with other systems, and at the same time also interacts with them. Players will have multiple dialogue choices to advance conversations, and they’re mostly free to answer them anytime. That said, sometimes not saying anything in itself will be its own dialogue option.

Depending on what you choose to do and say, conversations will change their course and the same goes for the rest of the game.

OVERSEE FIELD MISSIONS
You will be able to send agents off to do field missions. They are the only way players will be able to gather resources for base operations and collecting essential evidence and materials to advance the story. Each mission includes a number of objectives. Prior to launching a mission, the player will determine which objectives they want to focus on by assigning each objective a set amount of budget.

The higher the budget, the less time it takes and the more chance it will be successful.

WORLDBUILDING
A great number of surprises and details that provide much depth to the world of Growth Device is hidden within the game and waiting to be found.

Stay engaged, and you just may uncover a dark secret that resides near you.
